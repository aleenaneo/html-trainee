Rolex

Rolex SA is a Swiss luxury watch manufacturer based in Geneva, Switzerland. Founded by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London, England in 1905 as Wilsdorf and Davis, Rolex moved its base of operations to Geneva, Switzerland in 1919.
